REGISTRATION INFORMATION

  • Requirement of Documents for GST Registration for Individuals and Sole Proprietor’s
    1. Passport Size Photograph
    2. Copy of Owner’s PAN card
    3. Copy of Owner’s Aadhaar card
    4. Proof of business addresses (Copy of Electricity Bill / Rent /Lease Agreement / Municipal Khata Copy/ Property Tax Receipt/ Letter of Consent Etc.)
    5. Rent receipt with NOC (In case of expired agreement)
    6. Letter of Authorisation in case of Appointment of Authorised Signatory
    7. Valid Email Address
    8. Valid Mobile Number
  • Requirement of Documents for GST Registration for Partnerships and LLPs
    1. Partnership deed
    2. PAN cards of All Partners
    3. Photographs of All Partners
    4. Address proof of All Partners ( Copy of Aaadhar Etc)
    5. Letter of Declaration for Auth. signatory (Copy of Resolution passed by BoD/ Managing Committee and Acceptance letter)
    6. Proof of business addresses (Copy of Electricity Bill / Rent / Lease Agreement / Municipal Khata Copy/ Property Tax Receipt/ Letter of Consent Etc.)
    7. Rent receipt with NOC (In case of expired agreement)
    8. Valid Email Address of All Partners
    9. Valid Mobile Number of All Partners
  • Requirement of Documents for GST Registration for Companies
    1. PAN card of Company
    2. Certificate of Incorporation
    3. PAN cards of All Director’s
    4. Photographs of All Director’s
    5. Address proof of All Director’s
    6. Letter of Declaration for Auth. signatory with valid resolution passed (Copy of Resolution passed by BoD / Managing Committee and Acceptance letter)
    7. Proof of business addresses (Copy of Electricity Bill / Rent / Lease Agreement / Municipal Khata Copy/ Property Tax Receipt/ Letter of Consent Etc.)
    8. Rent receipt with NOC (In case of expired agreement)
  • Requirement of Documents for GST Registration for Hindu Undivided Families (HUFs)
    1. HUF’s PAN card
    2. Karta’s PAN card
    3. Owner’s photograph
    4. Letter of Auth. as a Proof of Appointment of Auth. Signatory with Copy of Resolution passed by BoD / Managing Committee and Acceptance letter
    5. Proof of business addresses (Copy of Electricity Bill / Rent / Lease Agreement / Municipal Khata Copy/ Property Tax Receipt/ Letter of Consent Etc.)
    6. Rent receipt with NOC (In case of expired agreement)
    7. Valid Email Address
    8. Valid Mobile Number
  • Requirement of Documents for GST Registration for Foreign Company
    1. Certificate for Establishment as a Proof of Constitution of Business
    2. Photo of the Promoter/ Partner
    3. Letter of Auth. for Proof of Appointment of Auth. Signatory with Copy of Resolution passed by BoD / Managing Committee and Acceptance letter
    4. Proof of business addresses (Copy of Electricity Bill / Rent / Lease Agreement / Municipal Khata Copy/ Property Tax Receipt/ Letter of Consent Etc.)
    5. Valid Email Address
    6. Valid Mobile Number
  • Requirement of Documents for GST Registration for Government Departments / Local Authorities / Public Sector Undertakings / Statutory Body
    1. Any proof substantiating Constitution as a Proof of Constitution of Business
    2. Photo of the Promoter/ Partner as a Proof of Authorised Authority
    3. Photo of the Authorised Signatory
    4. Letter of Auth. as a Proof of Appointment of Auth. Signatory with Copy of Resolution passed by BoD / Managing Committee and Acceptance letter
    5. Proof of business addresses (Copy of Electricity Bill / Rent / Lease Agreement / Municipal Khata Copy/ Property Tax Receipt/ Letter of Consent Etc.)
    6. Valid Email Address
    7. Valid Mobile Number
  • Requirement of Documents for GST Registration for Society / Club / Trust / AOP
    1. Registration Certificate as a Proof of Constitution of Business or proof substantiating Constitution
    2. Photo of the Promoter/ Partner as a Proof of Authorised Authority
    3. Photo of the Authorised Signatory
    4. Letter of Auth. as a Proof of Appointment of Auth. Signatory with Copy of Resolution passed by BoD / Managing Committee and Acceptance letter
    5. Proof of business addresses (Copy of Electricity Bill / Rent / Lease Agreement / Municipal Khata Copy/ Property Tax Receipt/ Letter of Consent Etc.)
    6. Valid Email Address
    7. Valid Mobile Number
